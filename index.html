"...and each separate dying ember wrought its ghost upon the floor..."

Deep underground lives an ancient race of creatures. For centuries they have left mankind alone, until one day, when the sky went black.

Dying Ember brings brutal combat into a beautiful interconnected underground isometric world. Fight massive creatures and harvest their corpses to upgrade weapons, armor and spells. Dying Ember requires precision combat, watching enemies for openings, and punishes you for making mistakes. There is no map, no quest marker, no regenerating health. This is not your nephew's video game.
